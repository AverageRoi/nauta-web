# Privacy Policy

Welcome to Nauta. Nauta is a Discord bot that provides several features such as data storing, fetching, and calculation of said data. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

## Information Collection

We collect the following Discord-related data:

> User: id and username.
Do we?
> Guild: id
We use it to distinguish servers so each one can have their own stored coordinates.
> Commands: Inputs
We collect the data you submit when using one of our slash commands so we can store the necessary fields such as the coordinates, aliases, and dimensions.

## Use of information

All of the information we collect is used to provide the bot functionality.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share and will never share your data with any third parties with only two exceptions:

- `Prisma`. We use Prisma as our database provider, in which case all of the information is private and protected.
- `Legal requirements`. If we are ever required by law to hand our information, we are obliged to do so.

## User control

Server members with specific role clearance in-server can delete any or all data in our database directly. If the bot is no longer in a server, all of the data from that server will be deleted in 5 days after it has left the server.

You can always contact us if you have any concerns or face any problems with data deletion.

## Data Security

Apart from all the privacy practices Discord makes use of, Prisma offers encryption at rest and in transit, tenant isolation, and claims to follow the GDPR standards.
